Data Analysis for Zepto E-commerce Inventory – SQL

Conducted end-to-end data analysis for Zepto, one of India’s fastest-growing e-commerce startups. Leveraged SQL to clean, explore, and analyze inventory data, extracting actionable insights on product availability, pricing, stock trends, and revenue to optimize business decisions.

Achievements:
Explored dataset, identified product categories, stock levels, and pricing patterns. Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to examine product categories, availability, and pricing inconsistencies.
Cleaned the dataset by handling null values, removing invalid entries, and converting pricing from paise to rupees.

Impact:
Data-driven analysis improved inventory management, pricing accuracy, and stock availability, enabling better business decisions and operational efficiency for the e-commerce platform.
